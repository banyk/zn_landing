about

I’m Zhanar Newton,

A Phlebotomist and Permanent Cosmetics Specialist. For over 15 years I have studied medicine and worked in the field of dermatology and aesthetic medicine. With a background in Dermatology, I am knowledgeable in skin disorders and dermatoscopy.

I am educated on workplace safety, universal precautions, cross contamination precautions, and keeping a sterile environment to protect my clients’ health and well-being.
